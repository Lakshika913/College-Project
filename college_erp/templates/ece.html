OBJECTIVE

B. Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering is a valued degree which aims to enhance the knowledge of the students and help them unveil fresh ideas to the world. The program focuses on furnishing the core skills of the students by providing hands-on -experience. In addition to the facilitation of quality academic environment, the program aims to help the students:

-Excel in their professional career, higher studies, and research.
-Generate new and innovative ideas.
-Become industry ready by participating in regular training programs.
-Serving the society as a whole.

BENEFITS

B.Tech Electronics and Communication degree has several benefits. After becoming a graduate in the said field, the candidates become worthy of highly remunerative job profiles. The degree-program is tremendously fruitful for those students who are fascinated by electronics, mathematics and physics. The companies which are associated with power electronics, telecommunication, mobile communication, satellite communication etc are always in search of competent aspirants hence widening the scope of the discipline. Some of the ancillary benefits:

-Workshops, seminars & conferences are organized in the department to gain relevant knowledge.
-Industrial Visits and training to provide exposure to the outside world.
-Training and placement drives are conducted by the college annually.
-Opportunities to join various clubs operated at the department level like Project club, Robotic club, IEDC etc..
-Participation in different competitions like CANSAT and SMART INDIA HACKATHON.

Career Opportunities

B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering prepares the candidates for a better tomorrow. A B.Tech (ECE) graduate can set his/her foot in private as well as public sector. In public and private companies, the candidates can work as Service engineers, Network Planning engineers, R&D Software engineers, Software Analysts, Technical directors etc. Some of the prestigious public sector companies which provide career opportunities to the ECE graduates include ISRO, DRDO, BSNL, BHEL, HPCL, NTPC, BEL, IES and many more. Distinguished private companies such as Microsoft, Accenture, Delloite, Siemens etc also desire eligible candidates from the said domain.
