OBJECTIVE

Information Technology program aims to equip the candidates with a thorough understanding of fundamentals as well as advanced principles of the area. Furthermore, the program intends to provide firsthand experience to the students as an attempt to transform them into well sought after IT professionals of tomorrow.
The program also focuses on:
-Building productive and Industry-ready IT Engineers.
-Inculcating students with effective communication and technical skills.
-Recognizing societal needs and constraints in professional endeavors.
-Delivering life-long learning to shape the upcoming leaders of tech-society.
-Providing innovative curriculum in order to ease the technological challenges.

BENEFITS

IT engineering program is fruitful in grasping thorough knowledge of the computer-based information systems. In the course of four years, the students get acquainted with the roles and responsibilities of an IT professional. Besides this, the analytical skills of the students are honed up so that they become ready for the future.

In addition to the foretold benefits, the program is also advantageous as it provides:
-In-depth knowledge of the latest technologies like Machine learning and Artificial intelligence
-Industry-led guest lectures and training

-Industry eccentric Curriculum.
-Extra focus on Database, computer networking, programming, web development, cloud computing, and business intelligence.

Career Opportunities

A student is never short of employment when IT engineering is being learned at the best Information Technology Engineering College. After completion of the course, an eligible student can work as Computer Programmer, Web Developer, Network Administrator, Database Administrator, System Administrator, Data Security Administrator, Information System manager, Computer Technician, Computer support Specialist etc.
