OBJECTIVE

The world we see today is running on wheels of machines everywhere around us, which have not only reduced the manual efforts to a large extent but has also helped students to have a great future ahead. JMIT is an institute that believes in delivering premium quality education to the students pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate programs in the stream of mechanical engineering. Take a glimpse at some of the primary objectives of B. Tech Mechanical Engineering:
-Help students learn more about the designing of machines with high precision
-Preparing students for successful careers in private industry, and government sector
-Apply, critical thinking and problem-solving skills in professional engineering practice or in non-engineering fields, such as law, medicine or business
-Make students identify, formulate and solve mechanical engineering problems

Career Opportunities

Mechanical Engineering Program is not confined to the boundaries of theoretical knowledge. It has so many more strands attached that unfurl amazing opportunities for the students doing B.Tech mechanical after they attain the degree. Whether the students are seeking job options in Maharatna and Navratna companies like NTPC, BHEL, GAIL, IOCL, NHPC, etc., or they want to join globally recognized companies, like MRF, Royal Enfield, etc. JMIT RADAUR helps them in getting employed at any of these with astounding packages. Despite saying this, it is important to remember that the future increment in the remuneration of the candidates will depend profusely on their skills, knowledge and experience. That is why it is important for students to select the mechanical engineering program only if they have interest in it. As far as the jobs are concerned, Mechanical engineers with degree in B.Tech Mechanical can serve the following designations and these are:

-Automotive Engineer
-Maintenance Engineer
-Control and Instrumentation Engineer
-Marine Engineer
-Mechanical Engineer
