OUR CAMPUS

We, as Mukand group has a vision not to increase the quantity of degree holders but to produce skilled graduates with quality education to enable them to be a productive part of the Indian economy. Mukand group has taken initiative and established advanced labs with skilled resources for students as centres of excellence. Following are the centres of excellence running:

  • Robotics - Electronics and Communications Engineering
  • Daikin Air Conditioning - Mechanical Engineering
  • Industrial Instrumentation & Automation - Electrical Engineering (Equipped by Rockwell Automation, Inc.)
  • Virtual Labs
  • Texas Instruments Innovation Center Lab (TIIC) - Electronics and Communications Engineering
  • Information & Communication Technology Skill Lab - Personality Development Department
