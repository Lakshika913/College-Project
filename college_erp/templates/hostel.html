Hostels – Home Away from Home

Choosing a College is also about choosing a new home. We ensure that students dwell in a healthy environment that helps them to grow and learn without any obstacle.

 
JMIT Campus has separate hostels for boys and girls with all modern facilities and attached mess.The hostel rooms are fully equipped with adequate furniture & fixtures. The hostel mess serves fresh and hygienic food. 24 Hour Power backup is available in the hostel. You will be provided with facilities like internet, and common room with TV & cable facility and a study room, magazines, etc. The hostels’ houses have a fully equipped gym and separate courts for BasketBall, and Badminton. The concerned and highly-experienced staff looks after the college assets and ensures that the hostel remains responsive all the time. Each hostel is managed by a full time warden working under the guidance of a Chief Warden.
